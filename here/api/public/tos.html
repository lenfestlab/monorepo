What is the App?

The App lets people know about local news that happens around them. The App knows a User’s location and sends the User an alert when the User gets near a place about which news content (“Content”) has been written, however we use motion detection to avoid sending you notifications while driving for added convenience and safety.  

 

When you install the App, you will be asked for permission by Lenfest to send you notifications, to enable the App to “Always Allow” access to your device’s location and to allow the app to detect your motion. (This is needed by Lenfest in order to send hyper-local news so the App functions as intended, and also not send alerts while you are driving.)
